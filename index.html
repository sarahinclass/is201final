Painting Made Easy

Getting your house painted is not just about enhancing its curb appeal; it's also a strategic investment in maintaining and increasing your property's value. A fresh coat of paint not only revitalizes the exterior, making it look well-cared-for and inviting, but it also protects your home from the elements, preventing damage such as rot and mold. Inside, new paint can transform rooms, creating a more pleasant living environment and even affecting mood positively. Whether you're looking to sell or simply want to enjoy your home more, painting is a relatively affordable way to achieve a significant upgrade in aesthetics and functionality.

Rocky Mountain Exterior Painting is an exterior painting company based in Meridian, Idaho. The best part: it is run completely by high school and college students! The company started as a sibling business and has now expanded to include cousins. Your choice to work with RMEP will not only result in a high quality paint job but will also support local teenagers in their college journey.

You might be thinking, "High school kids? I don't know about that." And we would say, "Yes, that's valid reason to be concerned." However, our employees each go through a training program from an experienced painter to ensure quality and precision. Also, at the end of each painting process, we will walk through the house with you to ensure that the paint job you got was exactly what you wanted. We believe that communication is key to success, thus we will communicate daily with you to ensure that in every step of the process you are receiving the quality you want.
