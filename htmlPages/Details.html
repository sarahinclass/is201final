Choosing Colors

Choosing the right color to paint your house is crucial as it significantly impacts the overall aesthetic appeal and ambiance of your home. The color sets the tone for both the exterior and interior spaces. A color consultation can be incredibly beneficial in this process, providing expert guidance tailored to your preferences, architectural style, and the surrounding environment. Professional color consultants consider factors such as natural light, existing fixtures, neighborhood trends, and personal taste to recommend harmonious color schemes that enhance your home's beauty and value. Their expertise ensures that the chosen colors not only complement each other but also create the desired atmosphere, making your house feel welcoming and visually appealing for years to come.








Paint Sheens

Exterior paints come in various sheens, each offering unique benefits and aesthetic qualities. Choosing the right sheen depends on the desired look, maintenance considerations, and the surface's condition.
  1. Flat
    • Provides a smooth, non-reflective surface that helps hide imperfections.
    • Stucco or siding with irregular textures
  2. Satin
    • Offers a subtle gloss, adding a bit of warmth, concealing minor flaws, and balancing durability and aesthetic appeal
    • Siding and trim
  3. Gloss
    • Highly reflective and extremely durable, providing a vibrant, polished look that enhances architectural details but also highlights surface imperfections
    • Doors, windows, and trim
In the bidding process, we will discuss options a sheen that will best match the aesthetic and material of your house.
